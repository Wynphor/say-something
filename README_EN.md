## What is this

Not a forum. Not a social platform.

This is a wall. You walk up, leave a sentence, a story, a thought — then you leave. Someone comes by later, reads it, and feels something shift.

Here you'll find whispered crushes, beautiful regrets, and people who just want to do good without asking for anything back.

Memes to make you laugh. Poetry to make you feel. Original or borrowed — it all counts.

---

## Two spaces

### 📖 The Wall

> Like notes pinned to a wall. You walk by, stop, and read.

Quiet. Settled. Like opening a book you didn't know you needed.

**How to submit:** Fork → add a `.md` in the right folder under `wall/` → open a PR → auto-merged

| Folder | About |
|:---:|:---|
| `wall/whisper/` | Whispered thoughts, late-night ramblings |
| `wall/story/` | Stories, memories, things that happened |
| `wall/unsaid/` | Things you wanted to say but didn't |
| `wall/poetry/` | Poetry, prose, anything literary |
| `wall/daily/` | Daily life — weather, cats, good food |
| `wall/gratitude/` | Gratitude, small kindnesses |
| `wall/archive/` | Borrowed words that moved you |

### 💬 Echo Wall

> Like a late-night comment section. You say something — someone hears you.

Immediate. Flowing. Like sending a letter and actually getting one back.

**How to join:** Open an Issue, write what's on your mind. Others can reply, or just leave a ❤️.

---

## Ground rules

Only kindness lives here.

No arguing, no sarcasm, no unsolicited judgment.

You can be plain. You can be poetic. You can be funny. You can say nothing at all. But respect every word someone chose to share — the way you'd want your own words respected.

See → [Community Covenant](CODE_OF_CONDUCT_EN.md)
