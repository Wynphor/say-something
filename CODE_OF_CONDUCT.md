# 社区公约 / Community Covenant

---

## 核心就一句话 / One rule

**这里只有善意。**
**Only kindness lives here.**

---

## ✅ 欢迎 / Welcome

- 写下你的心事、故事、回忆、日常碎片
- 玩梗抖机灵，博人一乐
- 文艺创作，寻找共鸣
- 转载触动过你的文字（请注明出处）
- 用 ❤️ 等 reaction 表达「我看到了，我懂」
- 在评论区说「我也有过这样的时刻」

---

- Share your thoughts, stories, memories, daily life
- Drop a joke, make someone laugh
- Write poetry, find your people
- Share someone else's words that moved you (credit the source)
- Leave a ❤️ to say "I saw this. I felt it too."
- Comment "I've been there too"

---

## ❌ 不欢迎 / Not welcome

- 攻击、讽刺、人身伤害
- 阴阳怪气、说教评判
- 争论对错、挑起对立
- 抄袭冒充原创
- 广告或无关推广

---

- Attacks, sarcasm, personal harm
- Passive-aggression, unsolicited lectures
- Debating who's right, stoking conflict
- Passing off others' work as your own
- Spam or self-promotion

---

## 💬 关于评论 / About comments

写下评论之前，想一想：

> 如果这段话是深夜你发在朋友圈，你希望收到这样的回复吗？

不确定该不该说的时候，**沉默也是一种善意。**

---

*Before you hit send, ask yourself:*

> *If you posted this at 2am on your story, would you want to receive this reply?*

*When in doubt, **silence is kindness too.***

---

## 🔨 处理方式 / How we handle things

仓库维护者会不定期巡查。发现不符合公约的内容，会直接删除，不另行通知。

不需要解释，不需要争论。这里不是一个需要辩论的地方。

---

*The maintainer checks in from time to time. Content that breaks this covenant gets removed — no notice, no debate.*

*No explanations needed. No arguments entertained. This isn't a place for that.*
