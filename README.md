## 🪧 这是什么 / What is this

这不是论坛，不是社交平台。

这是一面墙。每个人走过来，写下一句话、一段故事、一个念想，然后离开。后来的人路过，读到了，觉得被触碰了一下。

这里有少女心事，有美好的遗憾，有但行好事莫问前程。

玩梗是为了博人一乐，文艺是为了寻找共鸣，自创转载，皆为华章。

---

*Not a forum. Not a social platform.*

*This is a wall. You walk up, leave a sentence, a story, a thought — then you leave. Someone comes by later, reads it, and feels something shift.*

*Here you'll find whispered crushes, beautiful regrets, and people who just want to do good without asking for anything back.*

*Memes to make you laugh. Poetry to make you feel. Original or borrowed — it all counts.*

## 📦 两个空间 / Two spaces

### 📖 壁报墙 / The Wall

> 像贴在墙上的纸条，路过的人驻足阅读。

安静的。沉淀的。翻开就像一本书。

**投稿 / Submit：** Fork → 在 `wall/` 对应目录新建 `.md` 文件 → 提交 PR → 自动合并上墙

> *Like notes pinned to a wall. You walk by, stop, and read.*
>
> *Quiet. Settled. Like opening a book you didn't know you needed.*
>
> *Fork → add a `.md` in the right folder under `wall/` → open a PR → auto-merged.*

| 目录 / Folder | 说明 / About |
|:---:|:---|
| `wall/whisper/` | 心事 / Whispered thoughts |
| `wall/story/` | 故事 / Stories & memories |
| `wall/unsaid/` | 未说出口 / Things left unsaid |
| `wall/poetry/` | 诗 / Poetry & prose |
| `wall/daily/` | 日常 / Daily fragments |
| `wall/gratitude/` | 感恩 / Gratitude |
| `wall/archive/` | 摘录 / Collected words |

### 💬 回音壁 / Echo Wall

> 像深夜的评论区，你说了，有人听见。

即时的。流动的。像一封信寄出去了，真的有人回。

**参与 / Join：** 开一个 Issue，写下你想说的。别人可以在底下留言、用 ❤️ 共鸣。

> *Like a late-night comment section. You say something — someone hears you.*
>
> *Immediate. Flowing. Like sending a letter and actually getting one back.*
>
> *Open an Issue, write what's on your mind. Others can reply, or just leave a ❤️.*

## 🤝 约定 / Ground rules

这里只有善意。不争论对错，不阴阳怪气，不说教评判。

你可以质朴，可以文艺，可以幽默，可以沉默。但请尊重每一个人写下的文字，就像尊重你自己的心事。

> Only kindness lives here. No arguing, no sarcasm, no unsolicited judgment.
>
> You can be plain. You can be poetic. You can be funny. You can say nothing at all.
>
> But respect every word someone chose to share — the way you'd want your own words respected.

详见 / See → [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)
